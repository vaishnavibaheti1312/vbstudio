Designing spaces that feel like home

Introduction

VB Studio is an interior design practice focused on creating spaces that are refined, functional, and built to endure. Our work is driven by a structured approach, balancing design clarity, material understanding, and precise execution. Each project is carefully developed to ensure it performs as well as it looks.

From residential to commercial spaces, the studio delivers environments that are cohesive, considered, and aligned with the client's requirements.

“

VB Studio began with a single intention: to design spaces that truly reflect the people who live in them. Not trends. Not a fixed style. But real personalities, real lifestyles, and real stories.

For me, every project is deeply personal. I take the time to understand how you live, what matters to you, and how your space can support that in the most effortless way. Because good design goes beyond aesthetics — it lives in the details, the thought behind every decision, and the way a space feels when you live in it.

Vaishnavi Baheti

Founder & Principal Designer, VB studio
