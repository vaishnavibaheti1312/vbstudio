ABOUT US

Introduction

VB Studio is an interior design practice focused on creating spaces that are refined, functional, and built to endure. We focus on marrying aesthetics with functionality, using materials, lighting, and detailing that enhance everyday living. Every project is curated to reflect the client’s personality while delivering a seamless, elevated experience from concept to completion.

Our work is driven by a structured approach, balancing design clarity, material understanding, and precise execution. Each project is carefully developed to ensure it performs as well as it looks.

From residential to commercial spaces, the studio delivers environments that are cohesive, considered, and aligned with the client’s requirements.

“

VB Studio began with a simple intention: to design spaces that truly reflect the people who live in them. Not trends. Not a fixed style. But real personalities, real lifestyles, and real stories.

For me, every project is deeply personal. I take the time to understand how you live, what matters to you, and how your space can support that in the most effortless way. Because good design goes beyond aesthetics — it lies in the details, the thought behind every decision, and the way a space feels when you live in it.

Vaishnavi Baheti

Founder & Principal Designer, VB studio

OUR SERVICES

At VB Studio, we offer end-to-end interior design consultation for residential spaces, guiding clients from the very first concept through to final execution. Every decision is carefully considered, from space planning and layouts to finishes, lighting, and styling, ensuring that each element contributes to a harmonious whole.

Our approach combines practicality with aesthetic sensibility, creating interiors that are not only functional but also deeply personal. We work closely with clients to understand their lifestyle, preferences, and aspirations, translating them into spaces that feel refined, balanced, and cohesive.

Every project benefits from thoughtful guidance and meticulous attention to detail, resulting in interiors that are elegant, comfortable, and timeless.
