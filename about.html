About Us

VB Studio is a dynamic and innovative design firm, specializing in residential and commercial projects. Founded in 2021, we are a team of young and creative designers dedicated to providing comprehensive design and build solutions.
We offer end-to-end design and build services, ensuring seamless project execution from concept to completion. Our integrated approach allows us to manage every aspect of a project, from architectural design to interior detailing and construction management.

Vision Statement

At VB Studio, we believe in the power of design To enhance the human experience. Our vision is to create timeless, functional, and beautiful spaces that reflect our clients’ unique personalities. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of design, embracing new technologies, and delivering exceptional quality in every project.
Founder
Ar. Vaishnavi Baheti


As the Principal Architect and Founder of VB Studio, Vaishnavi Baheti is a passionate and driven entrepreneur dedicated to creating spaces that transform lives. With a Bachelor of Architecture degree from Dr. Bhanuben Nanavati College of Architecture, Vaishnavi has spent the past three years building a firm that prioritizes innovation, sustainability, and client satisfaction. Her design philosophy is rooted in the belief that every space should tell a unique story, blending beauty with functionality to elevate the human experience.
Through VB Studio, Vaishnavi has cultivated a team of young, talented designers, each passionate about pushing the boundaries of design while maintaining a deep respect for the environment and community. Under her leadership, the studio has grown into a trusted name in the interior design industry, known for delivering exceptional quality and creating spaces that are both timeless and deeply personal. Vaishnavi’s entrepreneurial spirit, coupled with her commitment to excellence, continues to shape the future of design at VB Studio.

Design Philosophy

VB Studio's design philosophy is rooted in the belief that spaces should be transformative, functional, and deeply personal. Every project is approached with a focus on innovation, sustainability, and a deep understanding of the client’s unique vision. Our aim is to create environments that not only look stunning but also enhance the way you live. We thoughtfully layer textures, light, and materials to craft spaces that are both timeless and welcoming, making every detail a reflection of quality and care
